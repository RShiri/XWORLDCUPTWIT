Power Index · Knockout Simulation

Prediction Lab

A single Power Index for the 32 teams in the Round of 32, blending each side's pre-tournament FIFA ranking with how they've actually played — recency-weighted form, finishing & shot-stopping quality, and squad quality. Those ratings then simulate every knockout tie to a predicted champion.

How the Power Index & predictions work

Every team starts from its FIFA/Coca-Cola World Ranking points (11 June 2026). Three capped adjustments sit on top: Form (±100, recency-weighted pts/GD/xGD per game plus finishing & shot-stopping), and Squad (±55, a best-XI rating proxy vs the field average).

For each knockout tie the Power Index gap becomes a win probability via an Elo-style curve (100-pt edge ≈ 64%). The higher-rated side is projected to advance; real results replace projections as ties are played. A deterministic favourites-advance projection, not a full bracket-probability sim.
